### Pick a color, any color
This marks the first of two uses of random color generation, which I took from [this 2009 article](https://martin.ankerl.com/2009/12/09/how-to-create-random-colors-programmatically/) by Martin Leitner-Ankerl. Fun fact: the same article introduced me to this cool fact:

> […] Furthermore, it is a property of the golden ratio, Φ, that each subsequent hash value divides the interval into which it falls according to the golden ratio!

A fact that I used to space out the background stars on the homepage more evenly!
